## Notes

- For apps not listed here, run the discovery phase (see SKILL.md Phase 1).
- App Store IDs can be verified at: `https://itunes.apple.com/lookup?id={id}&country=vn`
- Google Play IDs can be verified at: `https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id={id}&hl=vi&gl=VN`
- YouTube queries should include Vietnamese terms like `"đánh giá"`, `"review"`, `"có nên dùng"`, `"ứng dụng"` to surface VN-market content
